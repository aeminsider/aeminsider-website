// The mission

AEM has almost no good public content for people learning it.

The official docs get you started, and after that you're mostly on your own — piecing together Stack Overflow answers, half-finished blog posts, and tribal knowledge. AEM Insider exists to fix that: a clear, ordered path from "what even is AEM" to shipping production code, taught the way I wish someone had taught me.
