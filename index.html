// The format

No talking heads. Just the screen and the code.

Every episode is a tight, edited screen recording — built so you can follow along in your own AEM instance. No webcam, no intros, no "smash that like button."

01

Screen-first, always

You watch the IDE, the AEM author, and the code — exactly what you'd do at work. Nothing to distract from the build.
