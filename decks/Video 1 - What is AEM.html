// Chapter 01 · Adobe Ecosystem

Analytics & Target

Adobe Analytics
Your website's report card.

Tracks who visited, which pages they read, where they clicked, and where they left — so your content team makes smarter publishing decisions.

Adobe Target
Your personalization engine.

Two people on the same site can see different things. Target uses what Analytics knows to show each person relevant content — no extra pages needed.

// Chapter 01 · Adobe Ecosystem

Campaign & Commerce

Adobe Campaign
Your marketing comms platform.

Decides who to contact, what to say, and when — automatically. Connected to AEM, those emails look exactly like your website.

Adobe Commerce
The engine behind "Add to Cart."

Handles product listings, carts, checkout, payments, and orders. Connected to AEM, product pages feel like a seamless part of your brand.

Author, Publish & Dispatcher

Author
Back of house
  • Content team works here
  • Create & edit pages
  • Upload images & assets
  • Preview before publishing
  • Visitors never see this
Publish
Front of house
  • What visitors see
  • Optimized for speed
  • Scales to millions
  • Always on
Dispatcher
Cache layer
  • Caches pages
  • Reduces server load
  • Security layer
  • Handles traffic spikes
